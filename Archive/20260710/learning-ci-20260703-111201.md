# Learning: Final no-op apply EOF boundary

## Context
While triaging `data-platform-terragrunt-catalog` MR pipeline `7916495`, job `35936093`, the direct `dbx_external_locations` hook probe and workspace output probe from commit `54ca335` were inspected in the raw GitLab trace.

## Learning
The direct external-location hooks printed `I0720` and `I0721` during the initial apply, proving the hooks are wired and the Terraform module can start. In the final no-op root apply, `I0720`/`I0721` did not appear; `dbx_external_locations`, `dbx_sandbox_catalogs`, and `dbx_workspace_assignments` all failed with bare `EOF` after Terragrunt had evaluated locals, retrieved dependency outputs, copied files into `.terragrunt-cache`, and regenerated provider files, but before hooks or `terraform apply`.

## Future Use
For this EOF mode, do not blame external-location YAML mutation, guard scripts, missing workspace output, or Databricks provider execution unless a newer trace reaches hooks or Terraform. Treat the failure boundary as Terragrunt 0.99.4 generated-stack command plumbing for downstream dependency-output consumers on repeated or post-apply Terragrunt commands.
