# Learning: Sprint Review Jira Summaries

## Context
The user asked for a sprint review summary of Jira stories worked on during the last two weeks.

## Learning
For sprint review prep, summarize Jira work as a concise bullet list with the Jira ID, a short title or plain-English summary, and effect-focused bullets describing what was included or delivered. Prefer resolved stories in the sprint window when the user asks for work that was done.

## Future Use
When asked for a similar sprint review, use Jira `currentUser()` plus the relevant sprint/date window, focus on `resolutiondate` or status transitions to identify delivered work, and keep the output ticket-first and effect-oriented.
